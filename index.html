Rice Krispie Treats

By Sally's Baking Addiction

Cook time:
15 minutes
Serves:
10 servings (depending on size of pan)
  • What to Buy
  • How to Make It

Ingredients

You only need 5 simple ingredients for these treats.

  • Butter:
    The base of a great rice krispie is butter. You need 3/4 cup or 12 Tablespoons.
  • Marshmallows:
    For extra marshmallow-y-ness, you need 2 10-ounce bags of mini marshmallows. (The brand of your choice.)
  • Vanilla Extract:
    For extra flavor, only 1/2 teaspoon needed.
  • Rice Krispies Cereal:
    One box of cereal will do, but add as much or as little as you like.
  • Salt:
    Optional, but adding just one pinch adds a depth of flavor.

Preparation

The whole process takes no more than 15 minutes.

  1. Step 1:
    First, melt the butter and marshmallows in a big pot on the stove. Stir slowly. Once everything looks melted enough, you'll know when it is time to remove the pot from heat.
  2. Step 2:
    After the marshmallows and butter have been melted, it is time to move quickly. Add in your desired amount of rice krispie cereal and vanilla extract. If you choose to, also add in a pinch of salt.
  3. Step 3:
    Then, stir everything together.
  4. Step 4:
    Once everything is perfectly combined, you can now transfer your rice krispies into your desired pan.
  5. Step 5:
    To prevent sticking, line the pan with parchment paper or nonsick spray. Then begin to place the rice krispies in the pan.
  6. Step 6:
    To get your desired shape of your treats, press down gently and mold.
  7. Step 7:
    Once you have reached your desired shape, allow the treats to set for 1 hour.
  8. Step 8:
    After they have set, cut them, and enjoy!
